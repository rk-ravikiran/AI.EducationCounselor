### Financial Strategy

Given your budget consideration, we need to be strategic. Singapore's public universities offer competitive tuition fees.
*   **MOE Tuition Grant:** All Singaporean Permanent Residents and eligible international students can apply for the Ministry of Education (MOE) Tuition Grant, significantly reducing tuition fees.
*   **Need-Based Bursary:** Public universities offer various bursaries based on financial need. The "Need-Based Bursary" with an approx. amount of S$2,000 is a starting point, and there are often further government schemes available through the university's financial aid office.
*   **SkillsFuture Credit:** As a Singaporean, you can leverage SkillsFuture Credit for approved courses, which can be beneficial for skill development.
*   **CPF Education Scheme:** If you have a CPF Ordinary Account, you can consider using it for tuition fees, subject to prevailing rules and limits.
